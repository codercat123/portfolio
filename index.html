About Me.

Hi, I am Sheereen, an ambitious and creative professional seeking a career in Front-End development. My passion stems from both my aptitude and interest in developing visionary concepts into visible results. I am self-motivated with an instinct for innovation and design, a lifelong learner focused on being goal/growth-oriented and apply these values to everything I do. I have favorable interpersonal skills, carry a strong commitment and eagerness to fully immerse myself in the Front-End development industry.
